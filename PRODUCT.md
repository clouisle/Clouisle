# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Clouisle serves enterprise teams building, governing, and operating AI applications. Primary users include workspace members creating agents, workflows, and knowledge bases; administrators managing teams, access, models, auditability, integrations, and platform settings; knowledge owners curating documents and retrieval quality; and developers integrating APIs, tools, MCP capabilities, and embedded AI experiences.

Users are usually in a work context where they need confidence, traceability, and control more than spectacle. They may be monitoring usage, configuring production-facing AI behavior, debugging workflow execution, or preparing knowledge assets for team use.

## Product Purpose

Clouisle is an enterprise-grade AI agent, knowledge base, and workflow automation platform. It helps organizations build, deploy, monitor, and govern intelligent AI applications that can retrieve, reason, and execute across internal knowledge and external tools.

Success means users can understand system state quickly, configure AI capabilities safely, and trust that agents, workflows, knowledge bases, models, permissions, and operations remain observable and manageable at scale.

## Brand Personality

Professional, clear, and trustworthy. The interface should feel like durable AI infrastructure for serious teams: calm, legible, technically capable, and operationally reliable without becoming cold or cryptic.

## Anti-references

Do not make the product feel like a simple chatbot wrapper. Avoid flashy SaaS clichés such as excessive gradients, decorative glassmorphism, oversized vanity metrics, and marketing-style cards in operational screens. Also avoid over-engineered internal-tool density that makes business users feel excluded.

## Design Principles

1. Make operational state immediately legible.
2. Treat AI capabilities as governable infrastructure, not novelty demos.
3. Prefer calm confidence over visual spectacle.
4. Preserve enough density for expert workflows while keeping labels and hierarchy clear for non-developers.
5. Keep admin, platform, and user contexts visually disciplined and easy to distinguish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default to WCAG AA expectations. Preserve clear keyboard focus states, sufficient contrast in light and dark themes, semantic labels, reduced-motion compatibility where motion exists, and bilingual English/Simplified Chinese interface needs.
